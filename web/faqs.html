FAQ's

How does IbileMart work?

Ibile Mart is a neighborhood supermarket that promises the best home provisions and supplies. Our store is easily accessible with the best customer service in its environ. With a user-friendly website, our customers and get groceries, toiletries, beauty care and many more delivered to their doorsteps. Payment is fast and secure as we use the best payment platform in the country.

Where is IbileMart located?

Ibile Mart is located at 1, Garba Mohammed Lawal street, off Ogombo Road, Ajah, Lekki, Lagos, Nigeria. At the moment we have one supermarket as we look to grow and improve our eficiency and quality. Our webpage allows for our customers to order with ease from anywhere in lagos.

Can I Shop Online?

Yes!!!, We have made online shopping easy, you can now sit at home and get your fresh vegetables, wines, groceries just by placing your order on our website. Payment is fast and secure as we use the best payment platform in the country.

How secure is the website?

Ibilemart.com is 100% secured with the best SSL certificates on the web, we promise to keep your transactions safe and private. Ibilemart.com will at no point as for details of your card or bank as payment is done on an online payment platform and not our website.

How can i track my order?

Every order comes has a reference number. You can call our effective customer care to trace you order with the reference number or send us an email at cs@ibilemart.com

How long does the delivery take?

Time for delivery varies from the location of the customer but will be communicated as the order is made. All Deliveries are completed within 24 hours.

How do i make a complaint about the delivered package?

Please feel free to send all complaints to cs@ibilemart.com or call any of our customer care line.

Can i return delivered goods and get my money back?

If we deliver an incorrect or damaged item (damage that has been caused by us), we will happily replace the item at no additional cost to you. For us to replace such items, customers will need to notify the delivery driver of their observation at the point of delivery. Once customers have taken delivery of their order and signed confirming this, they take ownership of the items and ibilemart.com will be unable to accept any returns.
